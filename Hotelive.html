Hotelive

The aim of this project is to create common platform for booking a hotel room online, and generating a revenue through advertisement and pop-up screens.

Project Idea

To create common platform for booking a hotel room online, and generating a revenue through advertisement and pop-up screens.

Target Audience

Hotelive is meant for the millennials who love to travel and planning their vacations in new cities or they want to attend the business meeting and travel for different cities or locations.

  • Hotelive is a platform where the person can search and book hotel rooms in their budget plans.
  • The person can see the list of all hotels available as per the location entered while searching hotel rooms.
  • All descriptive information about hotel rooms, amenities they are providing to their customers is available in the details of the hotel room page.
  • Easily any person can search for hotels and book rooms with checking reviews of the hotel. Secure and fast payment modes are available.
